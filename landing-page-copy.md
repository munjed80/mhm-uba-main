**A:** Yes! UBA supports data import from CSV and JSON files. We also offer migration assistance for Pro and Agency plans to help you transfer from other tools.

**A:** You can cancel anytime with no penalties. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation, and you can export everything before leaving.

**A:** Absolutely. You can upgrade instantly or downgrade at the end of your billing period. Your data is preserved when changing plans.

### Q9: What payment methods do you accept?
**A:** We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. Purchase orders are available for Agency plans.

### Q14: Can multiple team members use one account?
